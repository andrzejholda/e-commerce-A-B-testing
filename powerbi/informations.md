# Power BI Dashboard Description

## Page 1 – Product Performance Overview

Purpose: Provide a high-level view of overall product performance and monetization.

Key metrics:
-Total Users
- Total Revenue
- Total Purchases
- ARPU (Average Revenue per User)
- ARPPU (Average Revenue per Paying User)

Business questions answered:

How many users interact with the product over time?
How revenue evolves across the analyzed period?
What is the average revenue generated per user?
Which countries generate the most users?
Which device type (mobile / desktop) generates the most revenue?

## Page 2 – Funnel Conversion Analysis

Purpose: Analyze user behavior across the product purchase funnel and identify conversion bottlenecks.

Key metrics:
- Users App Open
- Users View Product
- Users Add To Cart
- Users Purchase
- Conversion Rate View → Cart
- Conversion Rate Cart → Purchase
- Overall Funnel Conversion
- Drop-off Rate
  
Business questions answered:

How many users progress through each funnel stage?
Where do users drop off in the purchase journey?
Which stage of the funnel has the largest conversion loss?
How conversion rates change over time?
Do conversion rates differ by country or device?

## Page 3 – Cohort Retention Analysis

Purpose: Evaluate long-term user engagement and retention dynamics.

Key metrics:
- Active Users per Cohort
- Cohort Retention %

Key visualization:

Cohort Retention Matrix (cohort month × lifecycle month)
Business questions answered:
How well does the product retain users over time?
How engagement evolves after the first month of usage?
Which cohorts demonstrate the strongest retention?
Are there signs of declining engagement across cohorts?

## Page 4 – A/B Experiment Performance Analysis

Purpose: Evaluate the performance of two product variants and estimate their business impact.

Key metrics:
- Users per Variant
- Purchases per Variant
- Revenue per Variant
- Conversion Rate
- Conversion Uplift %
- Revenue Uplift %
- Estimated Revenue Impact

Business questions answered:

Which experiment variant achieves higher conversion?
Does the experiment lead to increased revenue per user?
What is the estimated financial impact of deploying the better variant?
Are experiment results consistent across devices or countries?

Dashboard Analytical Value

The dashboard combines several product analytics techniques to provide a comprehensive understanding of product performance:

- Product KPI monitoring

Funnel conversion analysis

Cohort-based retention tracking

Experiment-driven product optimization

Together, these analyses support data-driven product decisions aimed at improving conversion, retention, and revenue growth.
