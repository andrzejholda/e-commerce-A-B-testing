# Key Business Insights & Product Recommendations

## 1. High Mobile Usage

The majority of users interact with the product on mobile devices (~67k users), indicating that the mobile experience is the primary touchpoint for customer engagement.

### Recommendation

Prioritize mobile UX improvements, including:
- simplified navigation
- faster checkout flow
- mobile-optimized product pages

Improving mobile usability could directly impact conversion and revenue.

## 2. Geographic Concentration of Users

The largest user segment originates from France (~25k users), making it the most important market for the product.

### Recommendation

Focus growth initiatives in this region:
- localized promotions
- targeted marketing campaigns
- regional partnerships

Expanding in the strongest market may generate faster revenue growth than expanding into new markets.

## 3. Funnel Performance & Conversion Drop-off

Funnel analysis shows strong early-stage engagement:
- View → Add to Cart conversion: ~85%
- Cart → Purchase conversion: ~76%

The largest drop-off occurs at the final purchase stage, suggesting friction in the checkout process.

### Recommendation

Improve the checkout experience by:
- simplifying payment steps
- offering additional payment methods
- reducing checkout form complexity

Even small improvements in checkout conversion could significantly increase revenue.

## 4. Weak Long-Term Retention

Cohort analysis reveals strong initial engagement followed by a sharp decline after the first month.

This suggests that while the product successfully attracts users, it struggles to maintain long-term engagement.

### Recommendation

Introduce retention initiatives such as:
- improved onboarding experience
- personalized product recommendations
- re-engagement campaigns (email / push notifications)

Improving retention would increase customer lifetime value (LTV) and long-term revenue.

5. Positive A/B Experiment Results

The A/B experiment compared two product variants.

Variant B outperformed Variant A:
- Conversion A: ~64.9%
- Conversion B: ~68.3%
- Conversion uplift: ~5.2%

Variant B also generated higher revenue per user.

Estimated revenue impact

If Variant B is rolled out to all users, the experiment suggests a potential additional revenue impact of approximately $3.28M.

### Recommendation

Deploy Variant B globally, while continuing to monitor:
- conversion trends
- revenue per user
- regional experiment performance

--- 

# Strategic Product Takeaways

The analysis highlights three key areas for product growth:

1️⃣ Conversion optimization
Improve the checkout process to reduce purchase drop-off.

2️⃣ Retention improvement
Implement lifecycle engagement strategies to increase long-term user activity.

3️⃣ Experiment-driven development
Continue running controlled A/B experiments to validate product changes before full deployment.

Together, these initiatives can significantly improve both conversion performance and long-term revenue growth.
